About the Guest

Dr. Eleanor Sterling is a renowned cognitive neuroscientist and the founder of The Sterling Institute, a research firm dedicated to optimizing human performance in high-stakes environments. With over two decades of experience working with Fortune 500 CEOs, Special Forces operators, and Olympic athletes, she has developed a proprietary framework for decision-making under pressure.

Before founding her institute, Eleanor served as the Director of Behavioral Science at TechGiant Corp, where she led initiatives that reduced executive burnout by 40% while increasing creative output. Her research has been published in Nature, The Harvard Business Review, and Scientific American.

She is the best-selling author of "The Neural Executive" and her upcoming book, "Focus Capital," explores the economics of attention in the digital age. When she isn't in the lab or the boardroom, Eleanor is an avid alpinist, having summited three of the Seven Summits.
