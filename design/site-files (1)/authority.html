Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the interview process.

Do I need to come to the studio?
While we recommend the full Evolution Studios experience for the highest production value, we offer a high-fidelity Remote Production kit and virtual direction, ensuring you look broadcast-ready from your home or office.
How long does the process take?
The interview itself takes about 60-90 minutes. From recording to receiving your full suite of edited assets (podcast, magazine article, clips), the turnaround is typically 14-21 days.
Where will my interview be distributed?
Your audio interview will be distributed to all major podcast platforms (Apple, Spotify, Amazon, Google). Your video assets will be hosted on YouTube and provided to you for social media. The magazine feature is published in our network of digital business publications.
Can I use the content for ads?
Absolutely. You own 100% of the rights to the assets we create. Many clients see their highest ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) when running ads to these authority assets rather than a generic sales page.
