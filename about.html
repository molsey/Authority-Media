The Vision Behind Authority Media Network

Authority Media Network was founded by George Wright III, a 30-year entrepreneur, speaker, and media strategist who has built, scaled, and advised multiple seven- and eight-figure businesses.

As the host of The Daily Mastermind Podcast—with over 1,000 episodes—and a media executive behind several global brands and magazines, George has spent his career helping others rise above the noise to become industry leaders.

Through decades of experience in branding, publishing, marketing, and high-level partnerships, George saw a gap in the marketplace—most founders had incredible expertise but lacked the systems and access to become known authorities. Authority Media Network was created to close that gap.

Our mission is to give entrepreneurs and business owners the same level of media resources, strategic guidance, and credibility once reserved for the elite—so they can grow faster, influence more people, and gain access to the opportunities their expertise deserves.
